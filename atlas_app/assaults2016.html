Reported assaults per Denver Streets

A total number of 4,449 assaults were reported during 2014.
The streets are color coded from lowest (blue) to highest (red) number of reported assaults. The street segment with the higher number of reported assaults was 14th Street, between Glenarm Place & Tremont Place.
The relationship between centrality scores per street segment (independent variable X) & number of assaults per street segment (dependent variable Y) was analyzed using Spearman's & Pearson's correlation coefficients.
Spearman’s rho results: Closeness (-0.091087) | Reach ( 0.081127 ) | Betweenness (0.019013)
Pearson’s results: Closeness (-0.014637 ) | Reach ( 0.068604) | Betweenness ( 0.006622)
No significant linear or monotonic relationships between the centrality scores and number of assaults per street was found. Other statistical analysis would be required in order to investigate other kinds of relationships.
